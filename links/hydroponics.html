Autonomous Hydroponics Robot

Project Overview

  This hydroponics project is a part of the Robotics@Berkeley club. Hydroponics is a method of growing plants that involves using an artificial growing medium and a well-controlled growing environment. This project in particular takes the hydroponics aspect a bit further by automizing much of the process and incorporating spraying of nutrient-rich water â€“ a technique called aeroponics (rather than the traditional water-filled container method for hydroponics). For this setup, there is a controlled pump that isolates groups of nutrients and delivers them accordingly, sensors that monitor environment factors like water conductivity, pH level, and humidity.
